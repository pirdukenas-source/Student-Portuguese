This is a website for you to learn the basics of Portuguese (Portugal accent) through repetition of the most common verbs.

Why?

The fastest way to learn a language is through exposure by watching, listening to or reading content in the language. However, to be able to understand any of it, the learner has to have a basic understanding of the language to start consuming the content. A large quantity of Portuguese nouns are easily understood by English speakers. However, the absolute majority of the verbs, especially common ones, are different from English, making them the main roadblock to comprehension. Therefore, it is essential to learn the most 400 most common words in Portuguese to be able to continue learning.

Instructions

Open the "word list" above, then click on the audio player. It will begin reading the verbs on the right and example sentences with them on the left. The English translation isn't read, so before the Portuguese verb is read, glance at the translation. You can set the speed and volume. After a few repetitions of the entire list, you should start reading, listening to or watching native content. If words are unclear, search them up. After a bit of time, come back to the verbs and repeat them a few more times. Once you have over 10 repetitions and you are confindent that you know the majority of these verbs, you can stop listening and start exposing yourself to native content full-time.
